Employer Branding Aviation

Emirates: Adventure Awaits.

A cinematic global recruitment campaign built to inspire the next generation of pilots, shifting the story from technical skill to the emotional journey of flight.

RoleCampaign & Production Lead ForThe Emirates Group
01 · The objective

Reach beyond
the usual cockpit.

In a competitive market for aviation talent, Emirates needed to attract a more diverse global cohort, reaching past the traditional pilot demographic into the aspirations of a younger, more adventurous generation.

Global reach

Recruiting across six continents at once, in many languages.

Diversity mandate

A deliberate focus on under-represented regions and female aviators.

02 · Creative strategy

Off the instruments, onto the horizon.

The strategy centred on the human desire for discovery, framing a pilot's life as an epic narrative rather than a job description.

Cinematic

Visuals worth the cockpit

Anamorphic, high dynamic range cinematography that treated the role as an aspiration, not a vacancy.

Emotional

The pilot's own story

The narrative shifted to the internal monologue, the calm of a sunrise at altitude and the thrill of the destination.

The hook

Adventure, not just an airline

Positioning Emirates as a gateway to the world's most extraordinary places and to professional excellence.

The films

Real pilots, real journeys.

I led the production of the "Adventure Awaits" films, each following a real Emirates pilot.

03 · Outcome

A campaign that pulled, not pushed.

1,200+
applications generated through the campaign
Global
reach across continents and languages
Emotion
led the creative, where the category usually leads with specs
