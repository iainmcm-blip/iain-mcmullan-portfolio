The Objective

In an increasingly competitive aviation market, Emirates needed to attract a diverse cohort of global talent. The goal was to reach beyond the traditional pilot demographics and tap into the aspirations of young, tech-savvy, and adventurous individuals worldwide.

Global Reach

Recruiting from over 6 continents simultaneously.

Diversity Mandate

Focusing on underrepresented regions and female aviators.

Creative Strategy

We moved away from the cockpit instruments and onto the horizon. The strategy centered on the human desire for discovery.

movie

Cinematic Visuals

Utilizing anamorphic lenses and high-dynamic-range cinematography to frame the pilot's life as an epic narrative rather than a job description.

auto_stories

Emotional Storytelling

Shifting the narrative focus to the internal monologue of a pilot—the peace of the sunrise at 30,000 feet and the thrill of the destination.

The "Adventure" Hook

Positioning Emirates not just as an airline, but as a gateway to the world's most exotic locales and professional excellence.
