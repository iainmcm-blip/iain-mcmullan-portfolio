Emirates Flight Training Academy.

The full brand identity and go-to-market for the launch of one of the world's most advanced pilot training facilities, pairing Emirates' standard of luxury with the precision of ab-initio flight training.

RoleBrand & Go-to-Market Lead ForThe Emirates Group
01 · The brief

Launch a school as a statement of intent.

This was not just opening a training school. It was defining how Emirates would attract and shape the next generation of pilots. The brief was to create the academy's brand identity from the ground up and take it to market.

I owned the brand identity and the go-to-market strategy, aligning a high-tech training environment with the prestige of the Emirates name, then turning that into a recruitment proposition that filled the inaugural intake.

Inside the academy.
