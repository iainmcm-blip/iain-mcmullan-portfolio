Emirates Skywards: My Family.

A Skywards feature that lets members pool and share Miles across their household, so every family makes the most of what they earn.

RoleProject & Digital Marketing Lead WhenJan – Sep 2018
01 · The brief

Make Miles go further, together.

Loyalty Miles too often sit unused in individual accounts. The brief was to develop and launch a Skywards feature that lets a household pool and share the Miles they earn, so families reach rewards faster.

The insight

Miles are earned individually, but aspirations are shared. Letting families combine what they earn, without losing their own tier status, turns scattered balances into reward-worthy journeys and deepens loyalty across a whole household.

Individual to household

Shifting the unit of loyalty from a single member to the family they travel with.

End to end

From product definition through to the digital marketing launch across Skywards channels.

02 · How it works

One balance, the whole family.

Pool

Share your Miles

Contribute up to 100% of the Miles you earn on flights and partners, adjustable any time, down to 0%.

Up to 8

The whole household

A Family Head plus up to seven relatives, from spouses and children to parents and grandchildren.

Yours stays yours

Keep your tier

Tier Miles and existing balances stay personal. Only the Miles you choose to contribute are shared.

Sooner

Reach rewards faster

A combined balance unlocks Classic Reward flights, Cash+Miles and Instant Upgrades sooner.
