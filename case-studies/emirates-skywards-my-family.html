Emirates Skywards: My Family.

A new Emirates Skywards feature that lets members pool and share Miles across their family — so every household makes the most of what they earn.

My Role·Project & Digital Marketing Lead Jan – Sep 2018

Make Miles go further — together.

Loyalty Miles too often sit unused in individual accounts. The brief was to develop and launch a new Skywards feature that lets a household pool and share the Miles they earn, so families reach rewards faster and make the most of every Mile.

The Insight

Miles are earned individually but aspirations are shared. By letting families combine what they earn — without losing their own tier status — Skywards could turn scattered balances into reward-worthy journeys, and deepen loyalty across a whole household.

From Individual to Household

Shifting the unit of loyalty from a single member to the family they travel with.

End-to-End Delivery

From product definition through to the digital marketing launch across Skywards channels.
