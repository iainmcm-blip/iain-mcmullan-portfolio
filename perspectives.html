Perspectives

What I'm thinking about.

Longer-form thinking on brand, loyalty, AI and the craft of marketing leadership, drawn from 20 years of building them. New pieces added as they're written.

Loyalty

Loyalty isn't a points programme.

The brands that win treat loyalty as a relationship, not a transaction. Scaling Skywards past 20 million members, and launching My Family, taught me the real lever is emotional, not mathematical.

AI & Craft

AI won't replace marketers.

It will replace the marketing that was already replaceable. The leaders who thrive pair machine speed with human taste, using AI to reach the good idea faster, not to skip having one.

Brand vs. Performance

It's a false choice.

Across aviation, finance and hospitality I've watched teams burn budget defending brand against performance. The job is to build brands that also convert, and to measure both honestly.
